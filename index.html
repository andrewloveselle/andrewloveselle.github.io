The Bride and Groom

ANDREW

Andrew was born in Storm Lake, Iowa. He has two wonderful and funloving parents, Bill and Linda Withers. He has one lovely and talented younger sister, Emma, who is three years younger than him. The Withers have now resided in Waverly, Iowa for many years. Andrew plays baseball, plays the drums, watches movies, and takes naps! And he LOVES Elle (as you can tell by this website URL)! :)

ELLE

Elle was born in Fargo, North Dakota. She has two amazingly loving and supportive parents, Steven and Alicia Krogh. At the age of three, the three of them moved on down to Arizona, where her beautiful and witty little sister, Stephanie Krogh (aka Ickle Stephikins) was born two years later! After ten years down in the south, the Krogh family moved back up to Waukee, Iowa to be closer to family (and to have seasons again)! Elle loves to golf, sing, watch movies, and eat! And I guess she loves Andrew, too... :)

HOW WE MET

Elle had this friend Kristy that she went to high school with. Elle convinced Kristy to come to Luther, even though Kristy’s two older brothers went to Wartburg- and they both had Andrew’s dad as an advisor. Kristy met Andrew and asked Elle if she knew him; Elle said ‘Nope! But if you like him I’m sure I will, too’! So Kristy set up a double date one Friday night, and the four of them went to Elle’s room to watch Tangled... the rest was history!


THE ENGAGEMENT

After a very late double date night with Kristy and Andrew’s friend Blake, Andrew took the futon and Elle took the bed. Elle woke up to Andrew poking her in the back saying ‘Get up and get ready, I’ve got a surprise for you’! Andrew had been teasing Elle for the last few weeks, so Elle thought the surprise was McDonald’s.

Until…

Andrew wanted to incorporate our families into it, so he began by blindfolding Elle and driving around (which his dad did for his mom) until she was completely disoriented… which took about 30 seconds. After about 10 minutes, Andrew said ‘stay right here, I’ll be back’; he left the car for a few minutes, hopped back and drove around for less than a minute (which Elle found out afterwards was because he got a sudden rush of nerves), stopped again, and helped Elle out of the car.

When Elle took the blindfold off, they were standing next to the river (Elle’s dad proposed to her mom by his family’s lake) on the blanket that one of her Grandmothers quilted her for graduation. He proposed using an exact replica of the ring box that Chuck proposed to Sarah with in Elle’s favorite show (Chuck), and she said YES!

It started pouring rain the minute they kissed, and they ran back to the car. Sitting in the backseat, Andrew had a beautiful leather-bound Bible with ‘Andrew & Isabelle Withers’ engraved on the cover (which we are planning on having our pastor officiate from) and a watercolor painting of the view from Luther College of the Oneota Valley… with the river he proposed on running through it.
